Our Story

Why "Anasto"?

Anastomosis — the surgical connection of two separate structures. It's what surgeons do when they join blood vessels, reconnect intestines, or bridge neural pathways.

We chose this name because that's exactly what we do: create vital connections between clinicians who see problems every day and the engineers who have the skills to solve them. Like a surgical anastomosis, these connections need to be precise, secure, and built to last.
